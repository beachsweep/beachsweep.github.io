Our Mission:
Our mission is to connect the next generation with the environment, and the world around them, giving them exposure that may have been hindered due to technology. This will aid in forming a more environmentally aware genration who can bond with the environment and connect with nature.

The Problem and Solution:
Beaches are often dirty from the various tourists that take its beauty for granted, and harm natures equilibrium. We are using various technology such as Computer Vision, to use drones to detect trash on beaches in order to create a network of beaches that require cleaning
up. The drone operates in 20 minute intervals and can dock at a station to
recharge for 5 minutes, and gets ready to take its next flight. We aim to
cover at least 30 public beaches on the California coast by the start of
the 2023-2024 school year. Our CV algorithm detects trash from a
currently developing database of trash to easily detect the difference
between trash, sand, people, rocks, water, and any sort of branches that
wash up on shore.
