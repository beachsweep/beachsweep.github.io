Help The Fight Against Pollution

Using Drone Technology To Make Beaches Cleaner

Find Beaches Near You

Our Mission:
Our mission is to strongly bond the next generation of students with their environment, especially in a world where their connection to nature is convoluted by technology. A more environmentally aware generation will lead to a cleaner environment for our future

The Problem and Solution:
Beaches are often dirty from the various tourists that take its beauty for
granted, drastically harming the environment. The growing use of
technology among students and teens has disconnected younger
generations from their environment. BeachSweep is an application to
detect the location and size of clusters of trash along beaches to alert
nearby students to clean with the incentive of gaining community
service hours. This way, we have tackled the issue of polluted beaches
as well as environmentally-unaware students.

The Process (Clientside):
There are various components to our device. As mentioned before, we have the charging dock, the actual drone module, as well as the interactive map that the user can use to find beaches nearby that need to be cleaned up. The drones run automatically and have a team of operators running them at all times. They are checked to make sure they are not a safety hazard and are not disturbing people.

There are two different users of our device: the people who run the beach who must maintain the device, and the user who uses the website to search for beach cleanups - the people who maintain the device would purchase the hardware and software together. The user who is looking for beach clean ups can select the beach on the interactive map that they would like to sign up for, and can schedule a time to help a clean up.  After said user participates in the clean up, they may recieve volunteer hours for this. This is a means of incentive to encourage kids to support their enivironment and hopefully spark interest in the related subjects.
